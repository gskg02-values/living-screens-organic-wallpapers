# 🌿 LIVING SCREENS: "THE GREEN SERIES"

**The World’s First Organic Digital Screen Sanctuary.**

### 🌪️ The Problem: Digital Exhaustion
Currently, the tech industry spends thousands of hours and millions of watts of electrical energy generating synthetic digital wallpapers. These heavy, digitally rendered graphics contribute to visual friction, battery drain, and cognitive fatigue. 

### 🌱 Our Intervention: The Architecture of the seen but not deeply seen
The GREEN Series is not a standard gallery of Photography. It is a deliberate visual intervention. We have engineered smartphone screens using exclusively raw, organic elements: vegetables, fruits, plants, trees and leaves. 

Our core objective is absolute zero visual strain. By capturing the deep, seasoned textures of nature, we provide a friction-free resting place for the exhausted mind—a quiet space away from the noise and standard definitions of smart phone screen success. 

### 👁️ The Illusion of Rendering
When you first view a Living Screen, it creates an immediate sense of quiet confusion: *Is this a high-end 3D digital render, or is it real?* There lies our effort—the difficulty in sensing how this screen was developed. There are no synthetic pixels here, only the profound, temporary forms of nature, documented and preserved. As you scroll through the textures, you begin to feel the difference. Organic silence replaces digital noise.

### 🌍 Conservation of Energy
Our methodology is driven by a dual philosophy of conservation:
1. **Cognitive Conservation:** Dark, natural, screen-optimized textures that demand zero effort from the user's eyes, allowing app icons to breathe without visual clutter.
2. **Planetary Conservation:** A commitment to creating breathtaking digital aesthetics using the most sustainable resource available—deeply observed nature. 
3. **OLED power consumption:** Dark simple nature screens help reduce phones systems power consumption. 
4. **Space Consumption:** We have kept the size of screen as minimum as possible but with an eye on detailing & comfort in not interfering with the app icons & readability. Our Screens quietly sits at the background.
We invite you to experience the GREEN Series on your own devices. Download the screens, reduce your visual fatigue and join us in our effort to care & protect our planet in every means possible.
